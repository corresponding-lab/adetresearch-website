Titles such as Member, Associate Member, Affiliate, Collaborator, and Contributor indicate different levels of involvement and contribution within ADET Research Group projects. These titles are provided solely for academic and research context and do not imply equal levels of participation, responsibility, or authorship contribution.

Membership titles may evolve over time to reflect each individual's actual role and contribution as projects progress.
