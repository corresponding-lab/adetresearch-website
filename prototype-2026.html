Project III – Participant Information Sheet (PIS)
About ADET Research Group
Who we are
Founded in 2023, ADET Research Group is an independent, self-funded initiative based in Thailand. It brings together researchers across disciplines to conduct research with integrity, generating evidence across health, behavioral, and care systems.
What we do
We generate evidence ranging from psychometric validation to clinical and collaborative research — linking health, behavioral, and care systems to inform better practice and advance outcomes.
Our mission
To generate evidence with integrity and collaboration, transforming research into meaningful and lasting improvements in health, behavioral science, and society.
Our values
Independence
Operating without external funding or institutional influence.
Collaboration
Working with partners across disciplines and countries.
Integrity
Upholding ethical and transparent research.
Impact
Striving to generate evidence that leads to meaningful and lasting improvements.
Note on Activities:
All research activities under ADET Research Group are initiated and directed by the group, conducted without external funding, and operate independently from institutional programs.
Institutional Acknowledgment:
ADET Research Group is an independent, self-funded research initiative founded by personnel of the Faculty of Medicine Siriraj Hospital, Mahidol University.

To ensure transparency in academic activities, the establishment of ADET Research Group has been formally notified to the Faculty of Medicine Siriraj Hospital through an official memorandum submitted for acknowledgment. The communication was intended solely to inform the Faculty of the group’s existence and scope of activities as an independent research entity operating under its founder’s responsibility, without the use of institutional funds or resources. (Institutional memorandum submitted and received for acknowledgment on 30–31 October 2025, Faculty of Medicine Siriraj Hospital, Mahidol University.)
